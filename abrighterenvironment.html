"NARIMAT NGA AGLAWLAW"

(A Brighter Environment)


  • On October 3, 2019, “NariMATT nga Aglawlaw,” an information drive, was launched to instill responsible waste management among Ilokano communities.

  • Last December xx, the awarding of Cleanest and Greenest cities, municipalities, barangays, and schools was conducted. (*graphics of winners)
    • The Governor has also encouraged a “shame campaign” for the “dirtiest and brownest” cities, municipalities, barangays, and schools to serve as their wake up call.

  • Last January 22, the DENR–EMB Region I has already turned over the P25.5 Million cash assistance to PGIN for the construction of Plastic Recycling and Resource Recovery Facility at Brgy. Imelda, Marcos town.

  • The 202nd Founding Anniversary celebration of Ilocos Norte will center on the branding of “Renewable Energy Capital of the Philippines and also in Southeast Asia.
