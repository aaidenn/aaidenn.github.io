About Us

Learn More About Us

Our beautiful lands are located in the far north part of the Luzon, also one of the far most part of the Philppines.

  • Designated as Region I, occupying the northwestern section of Luzon and part of Central Luzon plain
  • The region comprises four provincesand one independent city.
  • It is bordered by the Cordillera Administrative Region to the east, the Cagayan Valley to the northeast and southeast, and the Central Luzon to the south. To the west lies the South China Sea.

The region was first inhabited by the aboriginal Negritos, before they were pushed by successive waves of Austronesian immigrants that penetrated the narrow coast. Tingguians (Igorot) in the interior, Ilocanos in the north, Pangasinenses in the south, and Zambals in the southwesternmost areas settled the region.
